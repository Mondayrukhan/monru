About THE WITCHER

The Witcher is a fantasy drama television series created by Lauren Schmidt Hissrich for Netflix. It is based on the book series of the same name
by Polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski. Set on a fictional, medieval-inspired landmass known as the Continent,
The Witcher explores the legend of Geralt of Rivia, Yennefer of Vengerberg and Princess Ciri.
It stars Henry Cavill, Anya Chalotra, and Freya Allan.
